[H]PERSECUTION[/H]

Persecution is generally a common experience of all believers. It is an act of suffering inflicted by men upon a believer for his/her benefits in Christ. Persecution is mainly from unbelievers and sometimes some believers who allow themselves to be used of the devil.

[LI](a) What persecution is not - [b] 1 Peter 4:15[/b] when it is caused by lack of growth, lack of knowledge, envy, unbelief, laziness and unfaithfulness. [/LI]
[LI](b) What is Persecution? [b] 1 Peter 4:12-14[/b]. When one is persecuted for righteousness.[/LI]

[LI]2. Cause of Persecution - [b] 1 John 3:19-21[/b], [b] Matthew 10:16-17[/b][/LI]
[LI](a) Standing against sin brings persecution [b] John 7:5-7[/b][/LI]
[LI](b) When a person refuses to conform with the world [b] Acts 4:16-20[/b][/LI]
[LI](c) When truth is preached (taught) [b] John 3:19[/b][/LI]

[S]3. Behavior during persecution[/S] - [b] Matthew 5:12,44-47[/b]
[LI](a) Do not lose your love, peace, joy and faith during persecution[/LI]
[LI](b) Persist in your righteousness[/LI]

[S]4. Blessing in persecution[/S] - [b] 2 Corinthians 4:17[/b]
[LI]God will open the windows of heaven and bless you.[/LI]
