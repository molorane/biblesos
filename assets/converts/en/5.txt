PERSECUTION

Persecution is generally a common experience of all believers. It is an act of suffering inflicted by men upon a believer for his/her benefits
in Christ.Persecution is mainly from unbelievers and sometimes some believers who allow themselves to be used of the devil.

1. (a) What persecution is not- 1 Peter 4:15. When it is caused by immaturity,ignorance,jealousy, unbelief, laziness, unfaithfulness.
   (b) What is persecution? 1 Peter 4:12-14. When one suffers for righteousness sake.

2. Causes of Persecution - John 3:19-21; Matthew 10:16-17.
   (a) Opposition to sin brings persecution John 7:5-7
   (b) When one fails to compromise with the world- Acts 4:16-20
   (c) When the truth is preached - John 3:19
3. Attitude during persecution- Matthew 5:12,44-47.
    Maintaining your love,peace,joy, and faith during persecution. But hold on to your integrity.
4.Blessing in persecution - 2 Corinthians 4:17
   God will open the windows of heaven and bless you.
