[H]Study Guidelines[/H]
[LI]Pray with a convert before starting the study.[/LI]
[LI]Prepare yourself to answer questions.[/LI]
[LI]Do not take more than 30 minutes on a study.[/LI]
[LI]Pray at the end.[/LI]
[LI]Allow the convert to take part in the study.[/LI]
[LI]Always remind a convert of the next meeting.[/LI]
