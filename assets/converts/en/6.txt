STEADFASTNESS  
Acts 2:41-42,44,46,47a

It is not only enough to start on a journey or any venture but what really counts is the manner in which
it is continued. Salvation is just the beginning of a journey to the heavenly home. But your eternal salvation
depends on your continuity with the Lord.

Steadfastness is keeping firm to ones principles. It entails consistency in what you believe(2 Timothy 3:14). It
entails consistency in conforming to biblical standards of living(2 Timothy 2:4). It entails getting involved in
the work of God and continuing in it(Acts 2:42-47); 1 Corinthians 15:58). It entails keeping your relationship with
the Lord unbroken, fresh and growing(2 Timothy 2:9). It also entails being identified with the church(Acts 2:41;
Hebrews 10:25).

Benefits of Steadfastness
1.It helps you to grow(2 Peter 3:18)
2.It helps you to keep standing, even at the face of persecution and trials of faith(Romance 8:35-37).
3.It makes you to be fruitful in the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ(2 Peter 1:8).
4.It makes you to be the man/woman of God wants to be(2 Timothy 2:21).

It pays to be steadfast. So make up your mind today.Finally,let us ponder on 1 Corinthians 15:58 as we pray unto God.
Conclusion Matthew 24:13.
