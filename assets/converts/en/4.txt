[H]TEMPTATION[/H]
[B]Genesis 1-6[/B]

Temptation is an attraction to do evil. There are various means the devil can use to tempt an individual. It may be through the eyes, body, flesh (feelings), position, money, friends, wife, husband, and so forth.

[LI]1. Sources of temptation - Devil [b] Matthew 4:3-5[/b]; [b] Genesis 3:1[/b][/LI]
[LI]2. His purpose - [b] John 10:10[/b]; [b] 1 Peter 5:8[/b][/LI]
[S]3. Overcoming temptation[/S] - resist the tempter
[LI](a) Know the word of God (Promises) [b] Matthew 4:4,7,10,11[/b][/LI]
[LI](b) Watch in prayer [b] 1 Peter 5:8[/b][/LI]
[LI](c) Flee from temptations rather than enjoy them [b] Genesis 3:6[/b][/LI]
[LI](d) Withdraw from your sinful friends [b] 1 Corinthians 15:33[/b][/LI]

Temptation is not sin but yielding to it is sinful.
