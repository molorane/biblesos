TEMPTATION
Genesis 1-6

Temptation is an attraction to do evil. There are various means the devil can use to tempt an individual.
It may be through the eyes, body, flesh(Feelings), position, money, friends, wife, husband, and so forth.

1. Agents of temptation-The Devil :Matthew 4:3,5,8; Genesis 3:1
2. His purpose- John 10:10, 1 Peter 5:8
3. Overcoming Temptation
-Resist the tempter(devil)
-Know the word of God(promises) Matthew 4:4,7,10,11
-Be Prayerful and watchful 1 Peter 5:8
-Flee temptations instead of entertaining them Genesis 3:6
-Dissociate from old sinner friends 1 Corinthians 15:33

Temptation is not sin but yielding to it is sinful
