[H]True Family[/H]
Motto: [b] Galatians 3:26-29[/b]; [b] Matthew 12:46-50[/b]

Everyone longs to be part of a decent family with a good reputation! There are gifts and visible things that benefit all members of every family ([b] Ephesians 4:11-12[/b])

[S]Characteristics of a true family can be summarized as follows[/S]:
[LI]1. A true family is made up of people who understand that they share a common bond. As a result, they stay together at home.[/LI]
[LI]2. In a true home, adequate help is given to everyone especially in the time of that person's need![/LI]
[LI]3. Everyone within a true family has the right to be heard and not be criticized or ignored because of speaking the truth on issues discussed.[/LI]
[LI]4. Every member of a true family sees the family as a place of refuge, at the time they are lonely, friends failing them, business or anything else not going well.[/LI]
[LI]5. Members of the family have all the freedom to express their feelings or matters without fear that other members may not understand them ([b] 1 John 4:18[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]6. When a member is absent, others look for them. No member has peace until the missing member is found and returned to the family.[/LI]
[LI]7. Every member of a true family wants to help another live well in this world and live with God forever.[/LI]

This unique opportunity to enjoy the richer life in a True Family abounds in the weekly meetings held by the DEEPER LIFE BIBLE CHURCH in all locations. Please do not miss out!
