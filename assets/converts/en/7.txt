True Family
Ephesians 4:9-16; Matthew 12:46-50

Every man desires to be identified with a respectable and dignified family. There are gifts and properties which are meant
to profit each family member(Ephesians 4:11-12)

Qualities of a true family can be summarised as thus:
1.The True Family is made up of people who feel that there is a common link binding them together. Therefore, they are usually
 at home when they are with one another.
2. In the True Family, adequate care is given to each member especially in times of need.
3.Each member of the True Family has the opportunity to be listened to without being tagged a critic for speaking the truth on a
 matter.
4. Each member views the Family as a REFUGE - a place of abode,in times of loneliness, poor treatment from friends,business, or anything.
5.Members of th True Family have freedom to express themselves without the fear of being misinterpreted by other members( 1 John)
6.While a member is missing, the others search for him. Nobody is at peace until that member is found, and brought in the family again.
7.Each member of the True Family wants to help one another to live well on this EARTH and also live with God forever.
This unique opportunity to enjoy the richer life in a True Family abound in the weekly meetings held by the DEEPER LIFE BIBLE CHURCH in
all locations. Please do not miss out!
