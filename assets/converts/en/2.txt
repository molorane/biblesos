[H]Prayer - The mighty weapon[/H]

Prayer is the most vital and mighty weapon in the hand of a christian. There is no limitation to the believer in prayer to all that he wants in this life as well as in the life to come.

[S]1. What Prayer is not[/S]
[LI]Not reading or recitation from prayer book or reading Psalms only or praying in the name of angels or prophets.[/LI]

[S]2. What is Prayer?[/S]
[LI]It is simply asking and receiving from God. ([b] Matthew 7:7[/b]; [b] 21:22[/b])[/LI]

[S]3. Prayer of Faith[/S]
[LI]But without faith is impossible to please Him ([b] Hebrews 11:6[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]Faith laughs at impossibilities and counts all things possible ([b] Mark 11:24[/b]).[/LI]

[S]4. Time of praying[/S]
[LI]Daily, morning, afternoon, night, always.[/LI]
[LI]*Importance of quiet time must be highlighted ([b] Mark 1:35[/b], [b] 1 Thessalonians 5:17[/b]).[/LI]
