Prayer - The mighty weapon

Prayer is is the most vital and mighty weapon in the hand of a christian,There is no limitation to the believer
in prayer to all that he wants in this life as well as in the life to come.

1.What Prayer is not. Not reading or recitation from prayer book or reading Psalms only or praying in the name of 
angels or prophets.

2.What is Prayer? It is simply asking and receiving from God. (Matthew 7:7, 21:22).

3.Prayer of Faith   "But without faith is impossible to please Him..." (Hebrews 11:6)
                     Faith laughs at impossibilities and counts all things possible.Mark 11:24.

4.Time to pray Daily, in the morning, afternoon, night, all the time.
  *The importance of quite time must be explicitly emphasised. (Mark 1:35, 1 Thessalonians 5:17).

5. Examples of answered prayer
   (a) Hannah - 1 Samuel 1:27
   (b) Hezekiah - 2 Kings 19:19,20
   (c) Give two testimonies of answered prayers from the church.
   (d) Give two testimonies of answered prayers from your experience.
