Assurance Of Salvation
1 John 1:8,9; Ephesians 2:4-9

Many people after having repented of their sins and received Christ as their personal Lord and Saviour still lack
the assurance that their sins have been forgiven and that they are now children of God.The reason for this is that
such people want to experience a sensation on their bodies: they want to feel saved.

How then one have assurance of salvation?
(i) Repentance Ezekiel 18:20. Acts 3:19; Isaiah 55:7
(ii) Forgiveness 1 John 1:9; John 5:24; Romance 5:1
(iii) Confession Romance 10:9-13
(iv) Holy Ghost Witness Romance 8:16
(v) New Creature 2 Corinthians 5:17

A person does not have to FEEL saved. After a person has genuinely repented and received Christ, such a one has been
saved by GRACE. It is by FAITH (Ephesians 2:8,9)
