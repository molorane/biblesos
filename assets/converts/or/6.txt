[H]HO TIISETSA TUMELONG[/H]

Taba kholo ha se fela qaleho ea leeto kappa morero o itseng empa tsela eo ka eona ho tsoelloang pele. Pholoho ke qaleho ea leeto le eang lehaeng le leholimong. Feela pholoho ea hao ea bosafeleng e its'etlehile ka tsoelopele ea hau le Morena.

Ho tiisetsa ke ho lula o tiile tumelong ea hao.
[LI]E kenyelelitse ho tsoelapele ka seo motho a se lumetseng ([b] 2 Timotiea 3:14[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]E bolela hape ho tsoela pele taolehong ea bophelo ka mangolo ([b] 2 Timotiea 2:4[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]E bolela ho ba le kabelo ketsong ea mosebetsi oa Molimo le ho o tsoellisetsa ([b] Liketso 2:42-47[/b]; [b] 1 Bakorinthe 15:58[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]E bolela ho boloka kamano ea hao le MORENA e tsitsitse, e nchafetse e bile e ntse e hola ([b] 2 Timotiea 2:9[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]Hape ho ikamahanya le kereke ([b] Liketso 2:41[/b]; [b] Baheberu 10:25[/b])[/LI]

[S]Meputso ea tiisetso tumelong[/S]
[LI]1. E u thusa hore o hole ([b] 2 Peterose 3:18[/b])[/LI]
[LI]2. E u thusa o lule o eme le hara liteko tsa tumelo ea hau ([b] Baroma 8:35-37[/b])[/LI]
[LI]3. E u etsa o behe litholoana tsebong ea MORENA Jesu Kreste ([b] 2 Peterose 1:8[/b])[/LI]
[LI]4. E u etsa o be monna kappa mosali eo Molimo a mo ratang ([b] 2 Timotiea 2:21[/b])[/LI]

Ho molemo ho tiisetsa tumelong. Kahoo, etsa qeto. Qetollong ha re nahanisise [b] 1 Bakorinte 15:58[/b] 'me re rapele Molimo.

[S]Qetello[/S]: [b] Mattheu 24:13[/b]
