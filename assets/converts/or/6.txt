HO TIISETSA TUMELONG

Taba kholo ha se fela qaleho ea leeto kappa morero o itseng empa tsela eo ka eona ho tsoelloang pele.
Pholoho ke qaleho ea leeto le eang lehaeng le leholimong.
Feela pholoho ea hao ea bosafeleng e its'etlehile ka tsoelopele ea hau le Morena.

Ho tiisetsa ke ho lula o tiile tumelong ea hao.
E kenyelelitse ho tsoelapele ka seo motho a se lumetseng(2 Timotiea 3:14).
E bolela hape ho tsoela pele taolehong ea bophelo ka mangolo(2 Timotiea 2:4).
E bolela ho ba le kabelo ketsong ea mosebetsi oa Molimo le ho o tsoellisetsa(Liketso 2:42-47;1 Bakorinthe 15:58).
E bolela ho boloka kamano ea hao le MORENA e tsitsitse,e nchafetse e bile e ntse e hola (2 Timotiea 2:9).
Hape ho ikamahanya le kereke(Liketso 2:41;Baheberu 10:25)

Meputso ea tiisetso tumelong:
1. E u thusa hore o hole(2 Peterose 3:18)
2. E u thusa o lule o eme le hara liteko tsa tumelo ea hau (Baroma 8:35-37)
3. E u etsa o behe litholoana tsebong ea MORENA Jesu Kreste (2 Peterose 1:8)
4. E etsa o be monna kappa mosali eo Molimo a mo ratang (2 Timotiea 2:21)

Ho molemo ho tiisetsa tumelong.kahoo, etsa qeto. Qetollong ha re nahanisise 
1 Bakorinte 15:58 'me re rapele Molimo. Qetello: Mattheu 24:13
