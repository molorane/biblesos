[H]THAPELO SEBETSA SE MATLA[/H]

Thapelo ke sebetsa se matlahali sa bohlokoa letsohong la mokreste. Ka mantsoe a mang, ha ho thibelo ho molumeli thapelong ho ka fihlela sohle seo a se batlang bophelong bona esita le bo tlang.

[S]1. Seo thapelo eseng sona[/S]
[LI]Ha se ho bala kapa ho phethaka lithapelo, kapa ho bala Lipesalema feela, kapa ho rapela ka lebitso la manyeloi, kapa baprofeta.[/LI]

[S]2. Thapelo ke eng?[/S]
[LI]Ke feela ho kopa le ho fumana ho tsoang ho Molimo ([b]Mattheu 7:7[/b]; [b]21:22[/b])[/LI]

[S]3. Thapelo ea tumelo[/S]
[LI]Empa tumelo ha e le sieo ha ho kamoo Molimo a ka khahlisoang kateng ([b]Baheberu 11:6[/b]).[/LI]
[LI]Tumelo e t'sheha tse bonahalang li sitoa me e nka tsohle li ka etsoa ([b]Mareka 11:24[/b]).[/LI]

[S]4. Nako ea ho rapela[/S]
[LI]Letsatsi le letsatsi, hosing, mantsiboea, bosiu, kamehla.[/LI]
[LI]*Bohlokoa ba nako ea boikhutso bo tlameha ho hlakisoa ([b]Mareka 1:35[/b], [b]1 Bathessalonika 5:17[/B]).[/LI]

[S]5. Mehlala ea thapelo e arabetsoeng[/S]
[LI](a) Anna - [b]1 Samuele 1:27[/b][/LI]
[LI](b) Ezekiele - [b]2 Marena 19:19-20[/b][/LI]
[LI](c) Fana ka mapaki a thapelo e arabetsoeng ho tsoa kerekeng.[/LI]
[LI](d) Fana ka mapaki a thapelo e arabetsoeng bophelong ba hao.[/LI]
