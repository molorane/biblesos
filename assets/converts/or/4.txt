MOLEKO
Genese 3:1-6
     
Moleko ke khohelo ea ho etsa bobe. Mekhoa e mengata eo liabolosi a ka e sebelisang ho leka motho.
Ekaba ka mahlo,'mele,nama(Boikutlo),boemo,chelate,metsoalle,molekane,le tse ling.

1.Mehloli ea meleko-Liabolosi Mattheu 4:3-5; Genese 3:1
2. Sepheo sa hae- Johanne 10:10; 1 Peterose 5:8
3. Ho hlola moleko- hanela moleki
  (a) Tseba lentsoe la Molimo(Lipallo) Mattheu 4:4,7,10,11
  (b) Falimeha thapelong 1 Peterose 5:8
  (c) Balehela meleko ho na le ho e thabela Genese 3:6
  (d) Ikhule metsoalleng ea hao ea sebe 1 Bakorinte 15:33

Moleko ka bo-ona hase sebe empa sebe ke ho inehela molekong.
