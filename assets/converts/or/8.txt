[H]BOLELLA METSOALLE EA HAU[/H]
([b]Mareka 5:18-20[/b]; [b]Johanne 1:44-51[/b]; [b]Liketso 8:25-40[/b])

E mong le e mong ea bileng le kopano le Morena Jesu, o na le bopaki lokelang ho arolelanoa le ba lahlehileng sebeng. Re na le ho nahana hore metsoalle le ba re tsebang ba ke ke ba re mamela ‘me ka baka leo re khetha ho pakela bao re sa ba tsebeng ba hole le rona. Sena ke thuto e fosahetseng le thetso ea Diabolosi le mano ho bao re ba ratang.

Ka mabaka a mangata, likamano tsa rona le metsoalle le batho ba haufi le rona li etsa hore polelo ea rona ea evangeli ho bona e be bobebe.
[LI]Ea pele, kaha re phetse le ho sebetsa le bona, mapaki a rona a ba le moelelo hape aa ba hohela. Ba ka paka ka phetoho e etsahetseng maphelong a rona.[/LI]
[LI]Ea bobeli, re ba tseba haholo hore re ka khetha mekhoa e nepahetseng ea ho ba atamela le lithapeliso bakeng sa pholoho ea bona.[/LI]
[LI]Ea boraro, taba e tiileng ea phelisano le bona e re beha monyetleng holima balumeli ba bang ho ba fihlela.[/LI]
[LI]Ea bone, ke molato oo re nang le oona ho Kreste le ho bohle ho ba bolella evangeli. Re fuoe taelo ea ho bolella libopuoa tsohle evangeli.[/LI]

In preaching the gospel to our acquaintances and relations we should be courteous and polite. They deserve to be respected as we do to those unknown to us. We should not make their evils or known sins our focus of attack. We should not attack their personality either. We have to present Christ and God's plan of redemption and not to condemn. If we testify to them in love and back this up with prayer, many of them will believe and be saved ([b] Mattheu 28:18-20[/b], [b] Luka 19:13[/b]).
