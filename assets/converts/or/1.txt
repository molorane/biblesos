KHOLISEHO EA PHOLOHO
1 Johanne 1:8-9,Baefese 2:4-9
Batho ba bangata ka mora ho bakela libe tsa bona 'me ba amohela Kreste e le Mopholosi oa bona ba ntse ba hloka kholiseho ea hore libe tsa bona li ts'oaretsoe le hore ese ele bana ba Molimo.
Lebaka la sena ke hobane ba batla ho utloa boikutlo bo itseng 'meleng ea bona, ba batla ho ikutloa ba pholositsoe.

Motho a kaba le kholiseho ea pholoho joang?
(i) Pako-Ezekiele 18:20,Liketso 3:19,Esaia 55:7
(ii) Ts'oarelo-1 Johanne 1:9,Johanne 5:21,Baroma 5:1
(iii) Boipolelo-Baroma 10:9-13
(iv) Bopaki ba Moea o Halalelang Baroma 8:16
(v) 'Mopuoa e mocha-2 Bakorinte 5:17

Motho haa tlameha ho IKUTLOA a pholositsoe. Ha motho a bakile ele ka 'nete 'me a amohetse kreste, ea joalo o pholositsoe ka MOHAU ka Tumelo(Baefese 2:8,9).
