1. Rapela le molumeli e mocha pele thuto e qala
2. Itokisetse ho araba lipotso tsohle tse ka hlahang thutong.
3. Se nke nako e fetang metsotso e 30 thutong ka ngoe.
4. Rapela qetellong ea thuto.
5. Lumella molumeli oa hao ea mocha ho nka karolo thutong.
6. Kamehla hopotsa molumeli oa hao e mocha ka kopano e hlahlamang.
