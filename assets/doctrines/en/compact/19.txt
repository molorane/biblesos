[H]Christ’s Millennial Reign[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That Christ’s Millennial Reign is the 1,000 years literal reign of Jesus on earth, which will be ushered in by the coming of Jesus back to earth with ten thousands of His saints. At this time He will judge the nations that dwell upon the face of the earth - [b]Jude 14,15[/b]; [b]2 Thessalonians 1:7-10[/b]. During this time, the devil will be bound - [b]Revelation 20:2,3[/b]. It will be a reign of peace and blessing - [b]Isaiah 11:6-9[/b]; [b]65:25[/b]; [b]Hosea 2:18[/b]; [b]Zechariah 14:9-20[/b]; [b]Isaiah 2:2-4[/b].
