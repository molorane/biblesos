[H]Water Baptism[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That Water Baptism is essential to our obedience after reconciliation with God. Water Baptism is one immersion (not three) “In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost”, as Jesus commanded - [b]Matthew 28:19[/b]; [b]3:13-17[/b]; [b]Mark 16:15,16[/b]; [b]Acts 2:38[/b]; [b]8:38,39[/b]; [b]19:1-5[/b]; [b]Romans 6:4,5[/b].
