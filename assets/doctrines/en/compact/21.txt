[H]The New Heaven and The New Earth[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That the New Heaven and the New Earth “wherein dwelleth righteousness” will be made by God and the redeemed shall dwell therein with God forever. This present earth which has been polluted by sin will pass away after the Great White Throne Judgement - [b]Psalm 102:25,26[/b]; [b]Isaiah 51:6[/b]; [b]65:17[/b]; [b]Matthew 5:18[/b]; [b]24:35[/b]; [b]2 Peter 3:10-13[/b]; [b]Revelation 21:1[/b]. No unclean thing will be there. There, we shall know each other, our knowledge having been perfected. There will be no more curse upon anything. There will be no more night; the glory of the Lord will be the light thereof - [b]Isaiah 66:22[/b]; [b]2 Peter 3:12,13[/b]; [b]I Corinthians 13:12[/b]; [b]I John 3:2,3[/b]; [b]Revelation 21:1-7[/b]; [b]22:1-5[/b].
