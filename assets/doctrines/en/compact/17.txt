[H]The Great Tribulation[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That the Great Tribulation will occur after the Rapture and will be a time of terrible suffering on earth. It is also referred to as the time of “Jacob’s trouble”- [b]Matthew 24:21,22,29[/b]; [b]Revelation 9:16[/b]; [b]Mark 13:19[/b]; [b]2 Thessaloians 2:3-12[/b]; [b]Revelation 13[/b]. During this time, the Antichrist will take possession of this world for a reign of terror. He will not be a system or organization but a person - a supernatural, diabolical being, in the form of a man who will blaspheme and proclaim himself to be God ([b]Daniel 8:23-25[/b]; [b]2 Thessalonians 2:7-12[/b]; [b]Revelation 13:1-10[/b]). The Marriage Supper of the Lamb will take place above while the Tribulation continues on earth - [b]Revelation 19:1-10[/b].
