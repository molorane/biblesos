[H]The Resurrection of The Dead[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That the Resurrection of the dead is taught in the Bible as clearly as the immortality of the soul. Every individual who has ever lived will be resurrected, some to honour and glory and others to everlasting shame and contempt - [b]Job 19:25-27[/b]; [b]Psalm 71:20[/b]; [b]Isaiah 26:19[/b]; [b]Daniel 12:2[/b]; [b]John 5:28,29[/b]; [b]I Corinthians 15:12-57[/b]; [b]I Thessalonians 4:13-16[/b]; [b]Hebrews 6:1,2[/b]; [b]Philippians 3:8-11[/b]; [b]Revelation 20:4,6,12,13[/b].
