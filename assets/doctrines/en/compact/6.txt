[H]Restitution[/H]
[S]God’s Word teaches;[/S]
That Restitution is making amends for wrongs done against our fellow- men, restoring stolen things to their rightful owners, paying debts, giving back where one has defrauded, making confessions to the offended and apologizing to those slandered so as to have a conscience void of offence toward God and man - [b]Genesis 20:1-8,14-18[/b]; [b]Exodus 22:1-7[/b]; [b]Leviticicus 6:1- 7[/b]; [b]Numbers 5:6-8[/b]; [b]2 Samuel 12:1-6[/b]; [b]Proverbs 6:30,31[/b]; [b]Ezekiel 33:14- 16[/b]; [b]Matthew 5:23,24[/b]; [b]Luke 19:8,9[/b]; [b]Acts 23:1-5[/b]; [b]24:16[/b]; [b]James 4:17[/b].
