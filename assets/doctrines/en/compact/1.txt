[H]The Holy Bible[/H]
[S]God's Word teaches;[/S]
That the Holy Bible, consisting of 39 books of the Old Testament and 27 books of the New Testament, is the inspired Word of God. We take the Bible as final authority in all matters concerning Christian conduct and work - [b]2 Timothy 3:16,17[/b]; [b]Proverbs 30:5,6[/b]; [b]Revelation 22:18,19[/b].
