45 Bonang Ho Hlahile Marung - E. Casais

1 Bonang, ho hlahile marung
Ea shoetseng batho kgale,
O tla Khutla lehodimong
Ho phutha batho bohle.
Alleluya!
Dumelang, Alleluya!

2 Bahlabanedi ba Jesu
Ba bapile le eena,
Mahlo a bona ke metsu,
Ba loanela Morena.
Alleluya! Dumelang, Alleluya!

3 Mafatshe a thothomela,
Mafika a petsoha,
Le dinaledi tsa wela,
Letsatsi la fifala.
Alleluya! Dumelang, Alleluya!

4 Utlwang, ho hlajoa mokhosi
Wa ho tsosa ba shoeleng,
Ba mengoa ke Moahlodi,
Ba tsoa bohle mabitleng.
Alleluya! Dumellang, Alleluya.

5 Dumelang, bana ba me,
Lona bohle ba nthatang,
Sieang mobu, le phahame,
Tlong ho ’na, atamelang!
Alleluya! Dumelang, Alleluya!
