79 Morena, O Ba Etele - S. Rolland

1 Morena, u ba etele
Bana bohle ba lefifi,
Lesedi le ba chabele
Ka bophara ba lefatshe

2 Bophelo ke uena feela,
Empa batho ha ba tsebe.
Ba lahlehetsoe ke tsela,
Ba khelositsoe ke sebe.

3 Tadima bomadimabe
Joa ba sa tsebeng thapelo
U ba romele diabi
Tsa ditaba tsa bopelo.

4 Bahlanka ba botshepehi
Ba ke ba rongoe dichabeng;
Ho bolella balahlehi
Jesu ea ba lopolotseng.

5 Moea o latele Lentsoe
Naheng tsohle moole rongoang;
Ha le setse le jadiloe,
E be oona o nosetsang.

6 U phakise u bokelle
Bao u ikhethetseng bona;
Re tla ‘ne re ba rapella;
U re utloe, Ntat’a rona!
