38 Morena O Tsohile - A. Mabille

1 Morena o tsohile, – Alleluya!
O tsohile lebitleng; – Alleluya!
Poloko e hlahile, – Alleluya!
Ea batho ba timetseng. – Alleluya!

2 Morena o tsohile, – Alleluya!
Hodimo thabo e teng; – Alleluya!
Satane o soabile, – Alleluya!
Lefatsheng tshepo e teng. – Alleluya!

3 Morena o tsohile, – Alleluya!
Legodimo le butsoe; – Alleluya!
Ba haufi, le ba hole, – Alleluya!
Kenang, le lebeletsoe! – Alleluya!
