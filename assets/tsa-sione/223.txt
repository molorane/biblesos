223 Ntat'a Rona Mahodimong - S. Rolland

1 Ntat'a rona mahodimong,
Re o beela thapelo.
Re se re kena phomolong,
Ak'o re fe thobalo.
Morena, o re lebele,
Bosiu bo be bo se.

2 Se re balele melato
Ya letsatsi le fetileng;
O re tshoarele ka Jesu,
'Moloki ea re shwetseng.
Re ntse re utloa bohloko.
Ke tseo re di entseng.

3 O re robatse Modimo,
Ka ditshireletsong tsa hao;
Ditsietsing le tlokotsing,
Re boloke ka mohau,
Re be re tsohele kganyeng,
Bophelong bo sa feleng.
