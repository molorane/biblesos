26 Alleluya, Alleluya! - A. Mabille

1 Alleluya, Alleluya!
Ho bokwe ea tsoaletsweng batho,
O tlile lefatsheng la baetsadibe.
Alleluya, alleluya!
Tlong, a re tlatseng makgotla a mangeloi,
Re bokeng Modimo o hodimo-dimo.

2 Alleluya, alleluya!
Ho bokwe ea tsoaletsweng batho,
O tlile lefatsheng la baetsadibe.
Alleluya, alleluya!
Tlong, bohle re etse ditlatse tsethabisang,
Re boke kgotso e hlahetseng lefatshe.

3 Alleluya, alleluya!
Ho bokwe ea tsoaletsweng batho,
O tlile lefatsheng la baetsadibe.
Alleluya, alleluya!
Mafutsana, barui, marena le bafo,
Kaofela ba biletsoa ho tla hauheloa.
