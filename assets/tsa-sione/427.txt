427 O Modimo, Re Hloreha Hakaakang? - F. Coillard

1 O Modimo, re hloreha hakaakang?
Fatshe lena ke la mahlomola;
Ke bodiba ba dillo tse babang,
Ka matsatsi pelo dia babasela.
'Me joale, qetellong ya matshwenyeho
Ke lefu, moren'a matshabeho.

2 Ha o bona re tsherehane pelo,
Re tsukutloa ke ditsokotsane,
Tlo Jesu, re utlwele bohloko!
Re tshedise, Morena ea kgabane,
O laele, difefo di tla fela;
Har'a dillo, kgotso e tla hlaha.

3 Leha maru, mehodi ya meokgo
E thibane, 'me e re fifatsa,
Chabisa mechaeli ea kgotso
Dipelong tse ntsho; dia o lakatsa.
Le poboding hlahisa tsa nyakallo,
Le tetemong, matla a tumelo.

4 A o bokwe, Modimo, Ntat'a rona:
O mohau, o re hauhetse!
O re bone har'a bodiba boo,
O phaletse, 'me o re tshedisitse.
Re inoloa ke pallo tsa lerato,
Re hlapisoa hlekeng sa tumelo.
