33 Jesu O Tsohile Bafung! - T. Arbousset

1 Jesu o tsohile bafung!
Alleluya, Satan'o fentswe!
Mohale a hlaha bathong
Ho lopolla ba neng ba hlotswe.

2 Le kae lerumo la lefu?
Monamodi o le robile.
Mo-re-lefella-melato
A tsoa mobung a o rahile.

3 Lebitla, ho hlola ha hao
Ke hofe na? O se o hlotswe;
Se teng joale ke mohau
O beeloang ba neng ba tsoilwe.

4 'Mele o jaloa sesileng,
O mpa o tla tsoha letlotlong,
O phele ka ho sa feleng
Leseding le lehlohonolong.

5 Re ikgothatseng hona moo,
Ka ho tshepa Moren'a heso;
Re buletswe lehodimo,
Re lebelleng tsoso ya Jesu.
