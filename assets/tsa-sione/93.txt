93 Kgalefo Ya Hao, Morena - S. Rolland

1 Kgalefo ya hao, Morena,
E ntlohele,
O itshoare bohale,
O lese ho nkgalefela;
Kea fela
Tlas'a dikotlo tsa hao.

2 Metsu ya hao e ntlhabile
Ka bohale;
E kene le masapong.
Letsoho la hao le thata,
Le boima,
Na le tla nkisa lefung?

3 Nama ya ka e fedile ,
E omile,
Kamoo ke shweleng kateng.
Ke lemohile melato
E meholo
Ke e entseng ho wena.

4 Dibe tsa ka ke manyala,
Dia ntshita;
Letsoalo le nqetile,
Maqeb'a ka ke a kgale,
A bodile;
Kgale ke senyehile.

5 Morena, mpone meokgo
E bohloko
E sekang mahlong a ka.
Kea rapela, o mphodise,
O mphedise,
Ke tsebe ho iketla.
