60 Tlo, Moea o Halalelang - S. Rolland

1 Tlo Moea o Halalelang
Moe’a ‘nete, oa cheseho,
Kena dipelong tse batang
Di tukisoe ka lerato

2 O re etele, Mohloeki,
Ka dineo tse ho uena
O romiloe ke ‘Moloki
Ho tla re abela tsona

3 Kajeno, o re thabise
Ka tshepo le ka tumelo;
Matl’a hao a re nchafatse
Pelo, moea, le kelello

4 O hlahise ka ho rona
Lerato, khotso, le mohau;
Re beisoe ditholoana
Tse loketseng bana ba hao

5 Re be joale ka difate
Tse hlomiloeng pel’a metsi
Re nosetsoe, Moe’a Ntate
Ka matsatsi le matsatsi
