382 Jerusalema ea Phatsimang - F. Coillard

1 Jerusalema ea phatsimang,
Lefatshe la pallo,
O motle-motle hakaakang,
Motse wa phomolo !
Maru, dirame, difefo,
Ho wena ke tse leng siyo;
Se teng ke thabo, ke kgotso,
Motse wa phomolo.
Jerusalema, Jerusalema,
Motse wa phomolo!

2 Jerusalema ea ratehang,
Motse wa topollo;
Hae la Ntate le kgahlehang,
Motse wa phomolo!
Tlamo tse thata tsa sebe,
Ho wena ha ba di tsebe;
Ke tokollo, ke poloko
Motseng wa phomolo.
Jerusalema, Jerusalema,
Motse wa phomolo!

3 Jerusalema, ea kgabane
Motse wa lerato;
O motle, o hlophehile,
Motse wa phomolo!
Ha ke fihla, ho tla ba joang,
Nyakallo e tla ba kaakang
Ha ke o bona ka mahlo,
Motse wa phomolo.
Jerusalema, Jerusalema,
Motse wa phomolo!
