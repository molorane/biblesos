95 A Re Tshabeng Tsela e Mpe - S. Rolland

1 A re tshabeng tsela e mpe,
E tsamauoang ke ba babe,
E ba isang tahlehong;
A re keng re ba furalle,
Le mekgoa ya bona yohle
E hanoang lehodimong.
Bana beso,
E be Jesu
Re mo utloang,
Re mo ratang
Ka dipelo tse chesehang.

2 Ke een'a re lopolotseng,
Eena feela ea re shwetseng,
Re flhlele tshoarelo.
Re mohlape o baballoang,
Re Iinku tsee a di disang,
Tse disetsoang Modimo.
Bana beso,
E be Jesu
Re mo utloang,
Re mo ratang
Ka dipelo tse chesehang.

3 Hase lerato le batang,
Ke lerato le chesehang
Le mo isitseng lefung;
Madi a hae a lefile,
Rato la hae le latile
Dineo lehodimong.
Bana beso,
E be Jesu
Re mo utloang,
Re mo ratang
Ka dipelo tse chesehang.
