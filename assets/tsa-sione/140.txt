140 Dillo Tsa Mahlomola-pelo - E. Rolland

1 Dillo tsa mahlomola-pelo,
Meokgo e keleketlang,
Modimo o di ela hloko,
O bala phophi tse rothang.

2 Jesu, Mo-jara-mahlomola,
O a tsebile kaofela;
Pelo ya hae e re soabela
Mahlokong 'ohle a rona

3 Tlo, Moya, wena Motshedisi
Wa dipelo tse soabileng,
Re mamedise evangedi
Ya bophelo bo sa feleng
