29 Ke Soabisitswe Modimo, - S. Rolland

1 Ke soabisitswe Modimo,
Ke lefu la Jesu,
Ea imetsweng ke dikotlo
Tse loketseng batho.

2 Jesu ea se nang molato
O thakgiseditswe'ng?
Na hase bakeng sa batho,
Batho ba sitilweng?

3 O mokaakang ka mohau
Ho baetsadibe,
Ha o neile Mor'a hao
Ho shwela ba babe!

4 O bokwe, Ntate, ke bohle!
Ho roriswe Jesu!
Topollo e phethehile,
Re hloletswe lefu!
