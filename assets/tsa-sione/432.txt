432 Tsohang, Baahi Ba Lesotho - V. Ellenberger

1 Tsohang, baahi ba Lesotho,
Ba tsoileng fifing la kgale,
Ba pholositsweng ka mohlolo
O re makaditseng bohle.
Lemohang rato la Modimo,
O re ntshitseng har'a dintoa
Le botshabeho ba madimo,
Difaqane, bokaota!
Moya, moya wa 'nete,
Moya wa bophelo,
Re nchafatse bohle,
Re hole ka tumelo.

2 Modimo, re phela ka kgotso.
Re bile re tswela pele
Ho se kotsi kapa kgathatso;
Re rute ho se lebale.
Hobane ruri ke makalo
Ntswe la hao ha le tla fihla
Le re tlisetsa kgatholoho
Ka oa ba bang mokgathala!
Moya, moya ...

3 Re tla sebeletsa Modimo
Har'a leru la dipaki;
Re bonela mane hodimo
Ba nchafaditsweng ka madi.
Dinatla tsohle tsa tumelo,
Ba evangedi, pheellang!
Ke mang, ho rona ya ka kgutlang,
Ha re beetswe tse kalo?
Moya, moya ...

4 Modimo, totisa tumelo,
Re be le moya wa 'nete;
Re lokelwe ke kgauhelo
E newang ka Jesu Kreste.
Re be dipaki tse tshepehang
Fatsheng lena la Lesotho,
Ho se kamoo ho ka senyehang
Tlotlo le neilweng batho.
Moya, moya ...
