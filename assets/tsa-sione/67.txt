67 Nyakallang Lefatshe Lohle - S. Rolland

1 Nyakallang lefatshe lohle,
Lona ba senyehehileng;
Jesu o batla kahohle
Batho ba lahlehileng;
Bafetohe,
Ba sokolohe bobeng.

2 Molao wa Mmoloki o teng
Ho barutuwa kaofela,
Hore ba ise ditjhabeng
Ditaba tsa ho phela;
Batho bohle
Ba bolokehe ho shweng.

3 Eyang, le re, ke ba shwetse,
Ke ba lefetse dibe,
Le Satane ke mo hlotse,
Matl’a hae a fedile,
Sefapanong
Topollo e hlahile.

4 Utlwang lentswe le molemo
Le rometsweng ke Jesu;
Le le kuke ka tumelo,
Ke le phedisang batho;
Matl’a lona
A ka fetola pelo.

5 Tumisang jwale hosanna!
Modim’o re etetse,
Bophelo reya bo fumana,
Jesu o bo tlisitse.
Ba Sione,
Tlong, re mo dumedise.

6 Jesu, thusa lentswe la Hao,
Le hasane lefatsheng;
Bohle re hopole molao
Oo re o boleletsweng;
Re phakise
Ho bontsha ba lefifing.
