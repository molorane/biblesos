184 A Re Phokoleng Sefela - S. Rolland

1 A re phokoleng sefela
Se bokang ea neng a re shwele,
Re tle re nyollwe timelong.
O re batletse tshoarelo,
Lehlohonolo le bophelo
Lefung la hae sefapanong.
Letlotlo le kganya
Di be ho Konyana,
Alleluya
Re roriseng, re tumiseng
Jesu ea re lopolotseng.

2 Sediba sa ho pholoha,
'Moloki, re tla o leboha
Ka pelo tsohle tsa rona;
Metsi a mohau le matla
A re tsholohela ka mehla,
Re hlaphoheloa ke 'ona,
Letlotlo le kganya ...

3 Mong 'a rona, ea molemo,
Re tla o boka ka dilemo,
Le balopolotsweng bohle;
O nke dipelo tsa rona,
Re o ananela ka tsona.
O di hlophise ka botle
Letlotlo le kganya ...
