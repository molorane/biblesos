202 Tlong Re Busetseng Lerato - S. Rolland

1 Tlong, re busetseng lerato
Ho Jehova ea re entseng;
O re baballa ka dintho
Tse re phedisang lefatsheng.

2 Ho eena ho tsoa tumelo
Le dimpho tse ding kaofela;
Toko, topollo, tshoarelo,
Kgalalelo le ho phela,

3 Re hapilwe ke lerato
Lee re ratilweng ka lona;
Re kgethilwe ke Modimo
Ho sebeletsa Konyana.

4 Re tieeng kanong tsa rona,
Phadimehong le tumelong;
Re ke re lebelle eena
Ea tla re nkela phomolong.

5 Jesu, tiisa bana ba hao,
Ba amohetsweng mohlapeng;
O ba hodise ka mohau,
O ba boloke lesakeng.
