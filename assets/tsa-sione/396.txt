396 Dumela Bethel Wa Rona - F. Coillard

1 Dumela, Bethel wa rona,
Fika se setle sa thabo;
Tsatsi lena la Morena,
Le leholo, la phomolo.

2 Basepedi ba kgathetseng
Ke keledi tsa kgororo,
Ha re o fihlela tseleng,
E kaakang ho rona thabo!

3 Re sa o bonela hole,
Pelo tsa hlasimoloha;
Ha re o fihletse joale,
Se teng ke ho kgatholoha.

4 Tlong, tlong, bana ba Modimo,
Tlong, re bokeng Ntat'a rona !
Re baleng, re pheteng tshitso
Tsee re sitsitsweng ka tsona.

5 Ha re ntse re kgatholoha,
'Moho re bina sedutse;
Ruri, ho fihlela mona
Modimo o re thusitse.
