169 Na Letsatsi Le Tla Nchabela Neng - E. Casalis

1 Na letsatsi le tla nchabela neng,
Lee ke kenang lehodimong ka lona,
Ke rorise lebitso la Morena,
Ka motseng oo ke o hlolohetsweng?

2 Fatsheng lena, re ntse re lla hobe,
Re melekong, ha re lese ho kula;
Ha ho motho ea tsebang ho phekola,
Bohloko bo kopane le dibe.

3 Lehodimo le hana molato,
Lehodimong dillo tsohle dia fell.
Ha ho lefu; teng bohle ba tla phela,
Lehodimo le loketse batho.

4 Jesu, Jesu, itlele ka ho 'na,
O dule teng, ke lalele le wena;
O ntokise, ke phakise ho kena,
Ke o bone, joale k'a o mpona,
