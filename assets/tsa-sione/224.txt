224 Naha Tsohle Bokang Morena Jehova - T. Arbousset

1 Naha tsohle, bokang Morena Jehova,
Ke eena Modimo; binang alleluya!
O bopile batho, o hlodile lefatshe,
Maru, dinaledi, le kgwedi, le letsatsi.

2 Bokang, bokang Jesu, ke eena Morena;
O re lopolotse, binang alleluya!
Bao a ba shwetseng, ba mo nke, ba pholohe;
A tume dichabeng, bohle ba mo lebohe.

3 Bokang, bokang moya o motle, o mosa,
Moya o mokgethoa; binang alleluya!

4 Baena, tumisang bitso la Jehova;
Chaba tsa lefatshe, binang alleluya!
Khumamelang Ntate, Mora, le oona Moya,
Modimo o le mong o roriswe ka mehla!
