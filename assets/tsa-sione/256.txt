256 Rona Re Ratang Jesu - L. Duvoisin

1 Rona re ratang Jesu,
Re ahisaneng ka kgotso;
Rona re ratang Jesu,
Re rataneng, ba heso.

2 Ke eena Mong 'a rona,
Ea re ruileng kaofela;
Ke eena Mong 'a rona,
Ea re entseng baena.

3 Ke hloho ya phutheho,
Rona re ditho tsa yona;
Ke hloho ya phutheho,
E re disang hammoho.

4 Ke sefate sa veine.
See rona re leng makala;
Ke sefate sa veine,
Ka eena re kopane.

5 A re mo kgomareleng,
Re tle re kgomarelane;
A re mo kgomareleng,
Re phethehele bonngweng.
