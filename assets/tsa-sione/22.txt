22 A Re Bineng Kajeno - T. Arbousset

1 A re bineng kajeno,
Ka lentswe le ka pelo,
Mong 'a botle, Mong 'a mosa,
Ea bohlale, Ea matla,
Morena wa ka mehla;
O tlile ho re pholosa.

2 A hlaha Bethlehema,
A tsoalloa ka setaleng,
Ho re ruta bonyenyane.
Manģosa a Jehova
A phokola hosanna,
A boka Mor'a Davida.

3 Ho roriswe Modimo,
Moren'a lehodimo;
Kgotso e be har'a batho,
Batho ba tshepe mosa;
Jesu oa ba pholosa,
Ba mo thabele ka pelo.
