28 Lefu La Hao, Molopolli - T. Arbousset

1 Lefu la hao, Molopolli,
Ke le harolang matsoalo;
Ke makaditswe, Monghadi!
O imetswe ke'ng hakalo?

2 Na hase ka ho ipea
Sebakeng sa ba itshentseng,
Re bileng ra o bolaya
Ka dibe tsee re di entseng?

3 Mohla o neelang moya
Lefatshe la sisinyeha;
Tsatsi la na la fifala,
Le mabitla a buleha.

4 Sera se ne se thabile,
Se re: Jesu o timetse;
Athe, wena, o tsohile,
Sera sona o se hlotse.

5 Ahe, ahe, Molopolli!
Madi a hao a tshepeha;
Ahe, ahe, Mo-re-shwedi!
Ka wena re tla pholoha.
