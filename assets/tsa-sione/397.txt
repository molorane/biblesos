397 Lefifing la Mahlomola - F. Coillard

1 Lefifing la mahlomola,
Le la mapalesa-pelo,
Ha tshepo e ntahlehela
Ana o kae, wena Jesu?

2 Ha ke ntse ke tsila-tsila,
Ho tshabeha, ho le hotso,
Jo, ho ipata le tsela!
Ana o kae, wena Jesu?

3 Ke itshela ka dikgapha,
Kea sareloa, ke bohloko;
O Monghadi, kea baba.
Ana o kae, wena Jesu?

4 O Jesu, ke batla wena.
O tsebane le bohloko.
Hlaha feela, hoa lekana;
Ana o kae, wena Jesu?

5 Oho, chabisa ka ho 'na
Mechaeli ea mohau;
Kganya har'a fifi lena,
Hlaha, o apole maru.

6 Jesu wa me, o mphodise,
O tseba ho baba ha ka;
Diha kgotso, o nthabise,
O ntlhahisetse sebaka.
