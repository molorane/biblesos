30 Topollo, Lebitso Lena - T. Arbousset

1 Topollo, lebitso lena
Le letle hakaakang!
Tsebeng tsa rona ke pina,
Ke hlare se okang.

2 Re tletse dibe le tshabo,
Re wela diheleng;
Re mpa re tsosoa ke tshepo
E tsoang evangeding.

3 Lentswe le re lokolotse,
Re tsoa ka phetoho;
Tau ya Juda e hlotse,
Ho setse pholoho.

4 Topollo! Lebitso lena
Le tume dichabeng.
Mangeloi a Morena
A le bine kganyeng:
Tlotliso
Kganya, letlotlo le matla
Ke tsa Konyana ka mehla!
Mong 'a rona ke senatla
Alleluya, bokang Jesu!
