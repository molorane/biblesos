418 Ke Mofero Ke Bofifi Feela - F. Coillard

1 Ke mofero, ke bofifi feela
Tseleng tsa Jerusalema;
Ke dithako, ke lepalapala,
Ke motse wa Morena.
Bakgethoa, rapellang Jerusalema,
Mo rapelleng le hlomohile pelo;
Mo lleleng ka meokgo, ke kgutsana
Mots'o motle wa heso!

2 Pina tsa mekete di fedile
Motseng wa Jerusalema
Jo, masoabi a koahetse bohle,
Baholo le ka bana.
Bakgethoa, rapellang ...

3 Jo, motse o motle, o ratehang,
Motse wa Jerusalema,
Ho hlahile'ng ? o fetohile joang?
Jo, motse wa Morena!
Bakgethoa, rapellang ...

4 Akofa, Monghadi, hauhela
Motse wa Jerusalema,
O o tsose dithako kapele,
O kgutlele ho oona!
Bakgethoa, rapellang ...

5 Rea mo rapella, Jerusalema,
Re hlomohile dipelo,
Re lla ka meokgo, ke kgutsana,
Mots'o motle wa heso!
Bakgethoa, rapellang ...
