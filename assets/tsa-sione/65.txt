65 Tlo Moya o Halalelang - F. Ellenberger

1 Tlo Moya o Halalelang,
Motshedisi ea ratehang,
Tlo, o kene ho rona.
O re sitse ka tumelo,
Ka pako le boitelo;
Re nchafatswe ke wena.
Amen, amen!
Tlo ka matla, tlo kajeno,
Re etele ka mohau.

2 Tlo, Moya o Halalelang,
Mobonesi ea tshepehang,
Tlo, o re hlalefise;
O re kgantshetse ka sedi
Le matla la evangedi,
Fifi o le suthise.
Botle bohle
Re bo bone, le bohlale
Bo re tlale ka sebele.

3 Tlo, Moya wa Jesu Kreste,
Moalosi wa kereke;
Tlo, o re matlafatse.
Mefokolo e ho rona
E k'e fediswe ke wena;
Ak'o re halaletse.
Toko, hlweko
Le lerato e be tsona
Kgabiso tse ntle tsa rona.

4 Tlo, Moya wa kgalalelo,
Moanyesi wa tumelo,
Tlo, o re tsamaise;
E be wena ea re rutang,
Ea re hlohonolofatsang;
O fele o re buse.
Amen, amen!
Tlo ka matla, tlo kajeno,
Re etele ka mohau.
