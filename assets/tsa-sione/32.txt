32 Bonang Lerato La Jesu - T. Arbousset

1 Bonang lerato la Jesu,
Kamoo a re shwetseng lefu;
O shwetse batho ba sentseng,
Ba timetseng.

2 O tlohetse lehodimo,
Ke eena Mor'a Modimo;
Ke Konyana e hlabilweng,
A re 'mokeng.

3 Seo Jesu a se batlang
Ke pelo tse mo mamelang;
O di hlekela bophelo
Bo sa feleng.

4 Re rateng bohle 'Moloki
Ea re biletsang ho hlweka;
Mohla a tlang, re tla bina
Alleluya!
