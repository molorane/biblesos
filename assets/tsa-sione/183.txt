183 Atamelang Ho Jehova - T. Arbousset

1 Atamelang ho Jehova,
Le ‘mineleng alleluya!
Naha tsohle, mo roriseng,
Mo rateng, mo sebeletseng.

2 Ruri ke eena Modimo
O moholo, o molemo,
O re entseng, o re disang,
Modimo o re pholosang.

3 Thabelang bohle Morena,
Mo lebogeng, tshabang eena,
Itleleng moo a rapeloang,
Lona ba mo hloloheloang!

4 Hobane ke ya lereko,
O re hlolela meleko,
Mosa wa hae wa tlotlega,
Nnete tsa hae dia hloleha.
