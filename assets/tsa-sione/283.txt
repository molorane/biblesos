283 Mamelang Mantswe A Matle - A. Mabille

1 Mamelang mantswe a matle,
A binang ka sebakeng!
Na hase a mangeloi
A ahileng ka kganyeng!
A dumedisa ho tsoaloa
Ha 'Moloki wa batho;
A bina dialleluya,
Dialleluya feela!

2 Kganya e be ho Modimo
Mahodimong, hodimo,
Kgotso e be teng lefatsheng.
Batho ba hauhelwe!
Tsatsing lena le tsoaletswe,
Ka motseng wa Davida,
'Moloki, Kreste Morena,
Alleluya ka mehla!
