425 Lelala O Tswele Pele - F. Coillard

1 Lelala o tswele pele,
Moeti wa Morena.
O tseleng, se belaele,
O tla fihla le wena.
Leha o se o kgathetse,
O tsoile le ditlhabela,
Bona, o s'o atametse,
Kgothala, o ea fihla.

2 Lelala o tswele pele,
Tsa tsela di bohloko;
Dia kallana, ha di fele,
Tse o fedisang pelo.
Empa hase tsa ka mehla,
Ke tsa motsotso o le mong;
Eto le se le ea fela,
O fihle lehodimong.

3 Lelala o tswele pele,
O tsamaye ka thabo;
Masoabi o a hlakole,
O ele sefahleho.
Se paqame, mofokodi,
Oho, tsoha hle molota;
O setse o le haufi,
O qetile bothata.

4 Lelala o tswele pele,
Mohlori ea ratehang.
Ha o setse o fihlile,
O tla thaba hakaakang!
Tsa tsela joale di be kae,
Le tse o palesang pelo?
Phomola, o fihlile hae,
Moratuwa wa Jesu.
