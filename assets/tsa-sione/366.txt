366 Utloang Taba tse Molemo - F. Coillard

1 Utloang taba tse molemo,
Tse re sisimosang pelo.
Ke tsa rato la Modimo;
Utloang, o ratile batho!
Wena, mang le mang
Ea ka dumelang,
O tla newa bophelo
Bo sa feleng.

2 Ha re ntse re ikgantshitse,
Re le dira tsa Modimo,
E, o ikgoahlapaditse
Morena wa lehodimo.
Wena, mang le mang ...

3 Bathong, har'a mafutsana,
Dihlong, bohlokong, le tlaleng,
O ikentse wabo rona,
A rata ba mo hloileng.
Wena, mang le mang ...

4 Sefateng se rohakilweng,
A ea betana le lefu;
Madi a hae a tsholotsweng
A re lefella melato.
Wena, mang, le mang ...

5 E, lefung a re hloibila,
A re ruisa bophelo;
E, re se re pholohile,
Ke moo re binang topollo.
Wena, mang le mang ...
