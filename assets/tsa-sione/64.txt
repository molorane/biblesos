64 Moya mohlodi wa batho - S. Rolland

1 Moya, mohlodi wa batho,
O ba tshedisang dipelo,
O ikgapele mee'a rona.
Moy'a Modimo wa 'nete,
O re bontshe, o re rute,
Re tle re tsebe ho lemoha
Ketso tse phahameng tsa hao,
Letsa Jesu, Mong 'a mohau.

2 O re fe dipelo tse ntle,
Di hlophisise ka botle;
O re sitse ka tsa ho baka:
Boikokobetso bohle,
Le lerato, le cheseho,
Le metsamao ya ho loka.
O sebetse ka lereko,
Re tle re phele ka hlweko.

3 Moy'a Jesu, Moy'a matla,
Lefatshe ha le re batla,
O re thuse ho furalla;
O re tedise menate
E nyedisitsweng ke Ntate,
Ye re neng re e kgathalla.
Re tshabiswe ditlokotsi
Tse tla tlela ba lefatshe.
